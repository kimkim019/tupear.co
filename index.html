What is
'Tupear'?

Tupear is the first UK virtual study buddy platform aimed to get university students to connect and find a study partner in your course. We enable students to share resources, learn from each other and cultivate in your studies. With over 50,000 undergraduate courses, you can find a study buddy Tupear with. We want to hear from you! Our Features

Get to know what our main features are on the Tupear app.

Message

You can communicate your study partner through our messaging system . This allows you to voice record, text and attach files.

Resources

Users are able to purchase and sell resources for studying purposes.                                                                      

Video Call

You can video call with a partner studying the same course as you. Through this, you will be able to work with your partner virtually.

Match

This allows you to find a suitable study partner for you that meets your criteria.
